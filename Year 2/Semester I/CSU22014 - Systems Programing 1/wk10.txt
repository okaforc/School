Huffman coding
find two least frequent chars
create a compound character
repeat
example:
    - A = 30 times
    - E = 40 times
    - I = 15 times
    - O = 20 times
    - U = 10 times
        - A = 30
        - E = 40
        - I, U = 25
        - O = 20
            - A = 30
            - E = 40
            - O, (I, U) = 45
                - A, E = 70
                - O, (I, U) = 45
                    - (A, E), (O, (I, U)) = 115
